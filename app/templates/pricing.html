Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

What happens if I exceed my limits?

You'll receive a notification when you're close to your limits. Upgrade your plan to continue generating tests.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Start with our free plan and upgrade when you need more features or higher limits.

How does priority queuing work?

Higher-tier plans get priority in our generation queue, ensuring faster processing during peak times.
